## What was cut and why

| Dropped Keyword | Reason |
|---|---|
| `AI replacing workers`, `AI unemployment`, `AI automation jobs`, `AI replace me`, `AI job loss`, `robots replacing humans`, `AI hiring discrimination` | Redundant with "AI taking jobs" / "AI layoffs" — 70-80% overlap |
| `AI environment`, `AI climate change`, `AI electricity`, `data center environmental impact`, `AI power grid`, `AI sustainability` | Redundant with data centers/energy/water/carbon. "AI sustainability" returns greenwashing |
| `AI vs artists`, `AI generated art problem`, `AI plagiarism`, `AI copyright`, `protect artists from AI` | Redundant with "AI art theft" / "AI stealing art". Too formal or too vague |
| `AI surveillance`, `AI privacy`, `AI tracking`, `AI data collection`, `AI listening` | High noise — returns tutorials, news, neutral explainers. Replaced with vernacular equivalents |
| `AI cheating school`, `AI essay detection`, `students using AI` | Redundant with "ChatGPT cheating". "students using AI" is too neutral |
| `AI existential risk`, `AI alignment problem` | Academic jargon — near-zero TikTok results |
| `stop AI`, `AI safety concerns` | Redundant with "ban AI" / "AI dangerous" |
| `AI misinformation`, `AI manipulation` | Redundant with "AI deepfake" / "AI fake news" |
| `#AIbacklash`, `AI ruining everything`, `AI bubble`, `AI overhyped` | Too niche or mixed valence |

## What was added and why

| New Keyword | Gap it fills |
|---|---|
| `AI slop`, `AI spam`, `AI garbage` | Quality degradation — arguably the #1 backlash narrative, was completely missing |
| `Google AI ruined search`, `AI making internet worse` | Search/internet degradation — major everyday frustration |
| `AI customer service sucks`, `can't talk to a human` | Everyday frustration — high volume, authentic backlash |
| `ChatGPT getting worse`, `OpenAI bad`, `Copilot sucks` | Company/product-specific backlash — large content category |
| `#SupportHumanArtists`, `human made not AI` | Affirmative resistance — captures "pro-human" movement |
| `AI watching me` | Vernacular replacement for formal "AI surveillance" |
| `AI voice cloning danger` | Refined from "AI voice cloning" to bias toward backlash not demos |

## Collection Parameters

- **Platform**: TikTok + YouTube (via Bright Data)
- **Time filter**: Last 12 months (April 2025 – April 2026)
- **Limit per keyword**: 200 (TikTok), 200 (YouTube)
- **Expected raw yield**: ~8,000–16,000 videos per platform
- **Deduplication**: By `post_id` (TikTok) / `video_id` (YouTube)

## Estimated Bright Data Cost

- 42 keywords × ~200 results = ~8,400 records per platform
- ~16,800 total records × $0.0015/record = **~$25**
